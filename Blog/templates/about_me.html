About Me

I am a hacker who is passionate about web development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, mental health awareness, and intersectional feminism. I am a proponent of diversity and inclusivity in the technology industry and elsewhere, and believe that social progress is just as important as technological progress.

I am currently in the Honours Computer Science Co-op program at the University of Waterloo, and will be entering my 3A term in the fall of 2016. I hope to never stop learning, and to use my knowledge to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others.

I love meeting new people and having unique discussions. Please don't hesitate to get in touch and ask me anything, provide feedback, or just say hi!
