About This Site

This landing page is an ongoing project, and I am continually improving on it as a showcase of my web development experience.

Architecture

This landing page is hosted on Amazon S3, distributed via CloudFront, and served strictly over TLS with a Comodo SSL certificate. It also utilizes many techniques to decrease page load times, such as code minification and cache-friendly response headers.

In short, it is blazing fast and highly secure.

Search Engine Optimization

I have paid attention to many SEO-related details on this site in order to increase my page rank on Google. These details range from mobile-friendly favicons to image alt tags, and are best practices that search engines encourage websites to follow.

Design

I am a big proponent of minimalism in design, so I designed this site from the ground up to be very minimal (and very usable). I opted for a single-page design, and used Skeleton CSS to make it beautiful and responsive.

Instead of building a flashy, JavaScript-heavy front end with tonnes of bells and whistles, I kept it simple, maintainable, and fast.
